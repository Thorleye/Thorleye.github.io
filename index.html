About Me

I am a third-year computer science co-op student with a proficiency in web development and database management. I am a former manufacturing sales rep which has instilled in me the communication, time management and teamwork skills along with the technical skills of computer science.

Born and raised in Toronto, I earned my Honours BA in 2012 from the University of Toronto before returning in 2018 for the full stack development bootcamp program from the U of T School of Continuing Studies. I enrolled at Toronto Metropolitan University (formerly Ryerson in 2020) in computer science. I am a strong team member but can work independently and have technical skills ranging from full-stack web development CRUD development and React, SQL and noSQL languages, object-oriented and functional languages, and languages for AI such as Prolog.
