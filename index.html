About Me

I am Eric Thorley, a Toronto-based, goal oriented software engineer.

My philosophy revolves around creating efficient and profitable products that not only satisfy clients' wants and needs but also meet customer demands in the market. I am committed to delivering solutions that align with market trends and drive business growth.
