About Me

I am a fullstack developer with an emphasis on the back-end and former manufacturing sales representative. I've decided to change career paths and learn the skills of web development to give myself the skills needed to create new and innovative web applications.

Born and raised in Toronto, I earned my Honours BA in 2012 from the University of Toronto before returning in 2018 for the full stack development bootcamp program from the U of T School of Continuing Studies. I have done volunteer work with churches, the children's aid society, community outreach programs and the Heart and Stroke foundation. I have worked jobs in a few kitchens and my previous role as a sales rep at a steel company involved in the japanese automotive manufacturing process.
